Understanding Semantic HTML

By Jane Doe — Dec 30, 2025

Introduction

Semantic elements give meaning to your markup — they help browsers, search engines, and assistive tech understand structure.

Why use them?

They improve accessibility, SEO, and make your markup more maintainable.

Tags: HTML, Accessibility

When to use section vs article

Quick rules

If content can stand on its own, use article. If it's a subsection of a larger whole, use section.
