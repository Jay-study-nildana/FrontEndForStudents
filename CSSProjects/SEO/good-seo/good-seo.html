What is semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML uses elements that describe their meaning — e.g., <main>, <article>, <nav> — improving accessibility and SEO.

Why accessibility matters

Accessible pages reach more users and are generally better understood by search engines. Use proper headings, alt text, and labels.
